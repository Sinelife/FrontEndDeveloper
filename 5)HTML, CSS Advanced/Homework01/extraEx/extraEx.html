National University of "Kyiv-Mohyla Academy"

Description: The Kyiv-Mohyla Academy, the school's predecessor, was established in 1615. The NaUKMA is located on the Academy's grounds in the ancient Podil neighborhood. In 1991, it was re-organized, and teaching began the following year. NaUKMA has the highest level of accreditation as outlined by the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine, and is one of the thirteen educational institutions in Ukraine having a status of a research and autonomous university. NaUKMA takes part in numerous international university collaborations, such as the European University Association. The university is bilingual in Ukrainian and English. It is one of Ukraine’s few universities with internationally recognized diplomas

Vyacheslav Stepanovych Bryukhovetsky
Ukrainian teacher and statesman, Honorary President of the National University of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy (NaUKMA), Kiev, Hero of Ukraine. First and former rector of NaUKMA
