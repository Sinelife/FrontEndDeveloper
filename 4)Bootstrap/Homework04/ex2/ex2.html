Chino Kafuu

Chino Kafū (香風 智乃 Kafū Chino?) is one of the main protagonists of the Is the Order a Rabbit? manga series as well as its anime series. She is voiced by Inori Minase.

Megumin

Megumin (めぐみん) is an Arch Wizard of the Crimson Demon Clan in the Fantasy World, and is the first person to join Kazuma's party. She is one of the main characters of the KonoSuba series

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado (竈門かまど禰ね豆ず子こ Kamado Nezuko?) is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the two remaining members of the Kamado family. Formerly human, she was attacked and turned into a Demon by Muzan Kibutsuji. She is one of the main protagonists of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Krul Tepes

Krul Tepes (クルル・ツェペシ Kururu Tsepeshi?) is a third progenitor among the vampires and the former vampire queen of Japan. She is a secondary major character who is responsible for turning Mikaela Hyakuya and Mahiru Hīragi into vampires. She is the younger sister of Asuramaru and one of the highest ranking members of the Progenitor Council, being a third Progenitor.

Senko

Senko is the deuteragonist of Sewayaki Kitsune no Senko-san.Senko is an 800 year old kitsune girl with radiant orange fur with white tail tips and brown fox ear tip

Ram

Ram (ラム) is one of the twin maids working for Roswaal L Mathers.As the prodigal elder twin Ram was born with extraordinary talent for mana manipulation and a powerful body to match that made her the pride of the Oni village.

Rem

Rem (レム) is one of the twin maids working for Roswaal L Mathers. Rem lived with her father, mother, and her elder sister Ram in an Oni village.

Chiya

Chiya is the protagonist of the series Urara Meirochou. A girl who sometimes appears to have animal-like ears and a tail. She was raised in the woods by her mother's friend, Setsu. Having grown up alongside animals, she is quite agile and energetic.
